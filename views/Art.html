Being suspicious of ADHD, my parents sent me to learn drawing since I was 4 years old. The expression through colors and strokes attracted my attention immediately and I fell in love with the creation ever since. It becomes a lens through which I learn and understand the world. I have experimented with various media including acrylic, oil, sculpture, installation and digital. With the support of my art teacher Wendy Tai, I was able to host my first exhibition, theme of which is symbolism of insects. I explored the utilization of insects in expression of love, body and time. Combined with my passion for machine learning, I researched the application of it to art and generated random drawing tool. I also utilized deep dream network to generate processed images to show how machine sees the world. Some selected artworks of mine are shown below.
