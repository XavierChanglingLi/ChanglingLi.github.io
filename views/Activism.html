With my diverse identities and enthusiasm in machine learning, I realized the problem of AI bias and inspired me to explore possible solutions. In collaboration with the Education Department, I created a short zine to raise the awareness of biases in AI. I researched on the causes of biases from both algorithm design and historical data and arrived at the advocacy that incorporation of data ethics in computer science education is a possible solution. My belief in data ethics shaped my own research as well. I am confident that incorporating AI fairness will benefit the underrepresented groups. I believe biases in AI can be eliminated from the education and thus, we can bring the change to the society.
